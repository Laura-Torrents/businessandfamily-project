About us

We organize activities for you and your family!

On a business trip in London, Barcelona or Rome with your family? Your family has free time to explore whilst you are busy with your conference/ meetings and looking for the perfect itinerary? Look no further. We will provide you with the perfect places and activities to visit within your family's timeframe and budget. We also plan trips for solo travellers to enjoy after their busy workdays.

  • Cost effective- we provide you with the most cost effective trips/activities that will make your trip perfect.
  • Time saving- we can save your time and effort by planning the trip according to your specifications.
  • Personal touch - Our consultants are available every day of the year - we don't use call centres. A personal touch is as important to us as it is to you.

How we started

We noticed how people who travel often for business, miss out on quality family time and are tired of being away from their loved ones. Therefore we created a service that will make it easier and more enticing to travel for business with your family, having full faith that they are well entertained whilst you are busy with your work responsibilities. We aim to make travelling for business with your family exciting and entertaining for all.
